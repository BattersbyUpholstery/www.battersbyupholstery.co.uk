Our Mission

Here at Battersby Upholstery we have been offering a premium quality reupholstery service for more than 40 years. We pride ourselves on our commitment to provide a high quality professional service to our valued customers. We take on all types of jobs from small domestic work, bespoke design to larger contract projects. We have an extensive range of fabrics from the leading suppliers and we are happy to use a customer's own fabric.

In our retail show room we stock and supply ranges from Sherborne upholstery, searchlight lighting, mirror art mirrors and many of our own in house upholstery products.

Call in any time to inspect our workshops and workmanship, we will be happy to show you around and answer any queries!
