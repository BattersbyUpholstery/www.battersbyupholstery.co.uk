Reupholstery

We are all far too familiar with the scenario; the thing that we love the most suffer from the effects of age, but we don't want to part with them - instead we want to carry on using that special piece of furniture! Reupholstery gives you the option to do just that, and if you want to give your furniture the best possible treatment, then it makes sense to use Battersby Upholstery's specialist reupholstery service. This ensures you can maintain the integrity of your favourtie furniture and enjoy them for many years to come.

Our sofa and chair reupholstery service is suitable for all furniture ranges, and we can also provide reupholstery in a wide range of fabrics, with the option to provide your own fabric if preferred.

At Battersby Upholstery, we offer a high quality reupholstery service with an outstanding customer service at a competitive price. Please feel free to contact us to discuss your upholstery requirements, view fabric sample book or a free no obligation quotation.
